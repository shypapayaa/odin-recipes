Omelet recipe

Omelettes are egg-based dishes typical in French cuisine but used all over the world. They consist of folded beaten eggs, typically with a filling consisting of vegetables, cheese or meats.

Today, we will show you how to make a basic but delicious ham and cheese omelet.

Ingredients:

    2 eggs Salt and pepper Cheese Ham Green onions for garnishing

Steps:

    Firstly, crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until well combined. Add salt, pepper and any other seasoning to the eggs. Add the eggs to a hot pan and let them cook for a few seconds. Place the filling in the eggs and gently fold the omelette in half. \Carefully move the omelette onto a plate and add chopped green onions as a garnish.
