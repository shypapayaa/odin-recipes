Omelette recipe

Omelettes are egg-based dishes typical in French cuisine but used all over the world. They consist of folded beaten eggs, typically with a filling consisting of vegetables, cheese or meats.

Today, we will show you how to make a basic but delicious ham and cheese omelet.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Green onions for garnishing

Steps:

  1. Firstly, crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until well combined.
  2. Add salt, pepper and any other seasoning to the eggs.
  3. Add the eggs to a hot pan and let them cook for a few seconds.
  4. Place the filling in the eggs and gently fold the omelette in half.
  5. Carefully move the omelette onto a plate and add chopped green onions as a garnish.
