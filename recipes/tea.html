How to make the best tea

Tea is a beverage enjoyed all over the world, often served both hot and cold.

It consists of a blend of herbs steeped into boiling water, making the water retain the anti-inflammatory and healing properties of the herbs.

Things you will need:

  • Kettle
  • Water
  • Teabags or loose leaf tea
  • A mug
  • Honey

Steps:

  1. Pour some water into the kettle and turn it on.
  2. Let the water boil.
  3. Place the teabag inside the mug and pour the boiling water into the mug, letting the herbs steep for a few minutes.
  4. Remove the teabag and dispose of it.
  5. For added flavor and sweetness, add some drops of honey to the tea.
